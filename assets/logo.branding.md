# MouseKeyProxy Logo Branding Contract

The primary MouseKeyProxy brand is a hacker mouse typing on a keyboard at a desk surrounded by monitors.

Required visible elements:

- Hacker mouse character
- Keyboard under the mouse's paws
- Desk surface
- Multiple surrounding monitors
- Active typing posture
- Technical/hacker workstation mood without generic stock abstraction

Required asset properties:

- `assets/logo.png` must be an inspectable raster render at 512x512 pixels or larger.
- The asset must remain readable at tray icon scale and documentation scale.
- Generic mouse-only, keyboard-only, monitor-only, abstract dot, or network-node logos do not satisfy the requirement.
